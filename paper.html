Where to get Paper

Origami Shop

Based in France. They sell a wide range of papers including high quality handmade papers and high quality machine papers.
They often stock hard to find papers including Origamido paper.

Kami Paper

Based in Melbourne, Australia. They sell large sheets of handmade papers for various uses.

origami.com

Based in Sydney, Australia. They sell machine made papers including Tant paper, but only small/medium sheets (6 inches).

Origami Paper Shop

Based in Colorado, USA. They sell a wide range of both handmade and machine made papers, but not many option over 9 inch squares.
